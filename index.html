Kintan Saha

I am currently a third-year undergraduate (rising junior) pursuing a B.Tech in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru. My current GPA (till end of semester 4) is 9.3/10.0

My research interests lie in Reinforcement Learning, with a focus on Stochastic Approximation methods and establishing theoretical guarantees for learning algorithms, as well as in Computer Vision, particularly in 3D scene reconstruction and novel view synthesis. I have practical experience training Deep Reinforcement Learning algorithms across a range of environments, including Atari, MuJoCo, MiniGrid, and others.

In addition, I have contributed to peer-reviewed publications submitted to top-tier venues and bring a strong theoretical grounding, supported by advanced coursework and rigorous, application-driven research projects.

My core technical expertise spans across -

  • Core Tools: Python, PyTorch, Git, LaTeX, Docker.
  • Reinforcement Learning: Gym, Stable-Baselines3, TRL (HuggingFace).
  • Computer Vision: OpenCV, Transformers (HuggingFace), Diffusers.
  • General: TensorBoard, WandB, CUDA, Linux/Unix proficiency.
